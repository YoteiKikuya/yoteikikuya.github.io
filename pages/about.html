About Me
I’m a third-year student at Simon Fraser University’s School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT), with a focus on narrative design in multimedia. I specialize in digital art as a means to explore fictional worlds and their inhabitants, incorporating 3D modelling in Blender, photo editing tools and digital painting techniques in Krita to create rich and immersive work. Additionally, I have experience in game design, videography and graphic design. My ultimate goal is to become a part of a creative team working with games (both digital or tabletop) or film, to create engaging worlds for people to immerse themselves in and grow attached to. My work is inspired by the Dark Souls games, Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, Warhammer 40K and the illustrations of Moebius and Stanley Donwood.
