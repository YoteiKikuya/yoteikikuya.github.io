About Me
I’m a second-year student at Simon Fraser University’s School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT), with a focus on narrative design in multimedia. I specialize in 2D visual art as a means to explore fictional worlds and their inhabitants. I work in tandem with literature, and also dabble in music, film and graphic design. My ultimate goal is to become a part of a creative team working with video games or film, to create engaging worlds for people to immerse themselves in and grow attached to. My work is inspired by the Dark Souls games, Star Wars, Lord of the Rings and the illustrations of Moebius and Stanley Donwood.
