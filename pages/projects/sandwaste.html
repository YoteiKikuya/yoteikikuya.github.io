Sandwaste
A Journey Through the Scraps of Civilization


Purpose: Personal Team Project
Category: Video Game
Software: Unity, Krita, Figma
Date: 10/04/2025 - Present

A hand-drawn metroidvania by Yotei Kikuya and Fernando Valdivia Vidal. Guided by visions of abundance, you play as a Siltcast,an artificial being from times gone by as you navigate a desert valley poisoned by the industrial metropolis at its center. Travel through wind-swept crags, dry lakebeds and hidden citadels, helping the eccentric beings who now call this desert home.
