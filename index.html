Who I am

I’m Yotei, an illustrator, concept artist, game developer and narrative writer. I am an undergrad at Simon Fraser University’s School of Interactive Arts and Technology.

What I do

I specialize in employing narrative to bridge aesthetics and user interactions to develop engaging experiences. Analyzing all aspects of a project, I identify emergent thematic and experiential threads that help ground the complete picture.

What I Stand For

I believe that empathy is the most important pillar of design. Every decision is made with the audience’s emotions in mind. In a world that increasingly prioritizes pure mechanical efficiency, maintaining the spirit of human-centric art and design is more important than ever.
